The Problem:

  • Driver fatigue is a major contributing factor to road accidents worldwide. According to statistics, drowsy driving results in thousands of fatalities and injuries each year. Many drivers underestimate the dangers of driving while tired, leading to impaired judgment, slower reaction times, and an increased risk of accidents.

    • The Solution:

  • Our project, "Driver Drowsiness Detection," aims to address this critical issue by developing an innovative system capable of detecting signs of drowsiness in real-time. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, including machine learning algorithms and computer vision techniques, our solution monitors driver behavior and alerts them when signs of fatigue are detected.
